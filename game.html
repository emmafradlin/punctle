Instructions

  • Drag punctuation marks into the sentence. They will appear in the grid as you place them.
  • Once done, click Check Guess to submit your punctuation.
  • After submitting, click Next Guess to try again.
  • Keep guessing until you use all six tries or correctly punctuate the sentence to win.
  • Use the See in Sentence buttons to view your past guesses.
  • Green: Correct punctuation in the right place
  • Yellow: Correct punctuation in the wrong place
  • Gray: Punctuation not in the sentence
